About Me

Hello there! I'm Justin, a web development enthusiast based in Cabuyao, Laguna, Philippines. My path to the digital world wasn't exactly a straight line – my high school journey took me through the culinary arts and even beauty care! While I found a connection in the creativity of cooking, it was my introduction to ICT in senior high and later, web development in college, that truly sparked my interest.

That initial experience with basic HTML in notepad, creating those classic marquee effects and playing with colors, was surprisingly captivating. Although life took a detour with part-time work and other commitments (including a brief stint as a barista!), the idea of building something tangible and visually engaging online – a modern-day resume, if you will – stuck with me. The ability to showcase skills and creations for others to see is a significant motivator. While I'll admit debugging can test my patience, the sheer satisfaction of seeing a project finally come to life is incredibly rewarding.

My college journey has further fueled this exploration. Beyond the basics, I've dabbled in more complex projects like a heart disease risk assessment system using Python and Streamlit, a secure login system with PHP and SQL, and even a fun animal guessing game in Unity. These experiences, while diverse, have solidified my interest in the practical application of technology. Interestingly, my recent On-the-Job Training at Concentrix in Nuvali also ignited a fascination for the hardware side of tech, adding another layer to my learning goals.

When it comes to web development, I'm particularly drawn to the visual design and interactive elements – the "eye-candy" that captures people's attention. Outside the digital realm, I'm a big fan of good food (living in the Philippines, how could I not be?), enjoy strategizing in Mobile Legends, and cherish spending quality time with my girlfriend. I believe that even these seemingly unrelated interests can find their expression in the creative possibilities of web development.

While I might sometimes feel like I lean on AI as a learning tool (and who doesn't in this age?), I'm driven by a desire to be more than just "normal." I'm eager to solidify my skills, explore the hardware world, and ultimately contribute meaningfully to the tech landscape. This e-portfolio is a step in that journey, a place where I can showcase what I've built and who I am.
